About Project

Project Overview

Freshify is an AI-powered system designed to classify fruits as fresh or rotten. This project aims to assist farmers in making quick and informed decisions regarding their produce quality.

Key Problems

  • Prevents undetected hazardous gas leaks that could lead to fires, explosions, or poisoning.
  • Enables real-time tracking of gas levels even when users are not on-site.
  • Reduces risks by triggering safety measures (e.g., alarms, exhaust fans) automatically upon detecting dangerous gas levels.

    • Dataset Attributes

    The dataset contains images of six classes: Fresh Apple, Fresh Banana, Fresh Orange, Rotten Apple, Rotten Banana, and Rotten Orange. Each image is labeled and preprocessed for training the model.

    Model Configuration

    The model is built using TensorFlow and Keras. It consists of convolutional layers for feature extraction, pooling layers for dimensionality reduction, and dense layers for classification.
